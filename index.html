WHO WE ARE

DMV Precision Tools, established on 07 September 2009 in Chennai, is a trusted manufacturer specializing in precision tools, press tools, and industrial tooling solutions. Over the years, the company has built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality products that meet the evolving needs of the manufacturing and engineering industries.

Through consistent service, competitive pricing and dependable supply capabilities. DMV Precision Tools continues to build long-term relationships with clients and strives to be a trusted partner for precision tooling and engineering solutions in the manufacturing industry.

OUR MISSION

With a focus on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, DMV Precision Tools is committed to delivering durable and efficient products that support modern manufacturing processes. Our team combines technical knowledge with practical industry experience to ensure every product meets the required performance and quality standards.

WHAT WE OFFER

Our product range includes air cut-off tools, air plasma cutters, boring head shanks, press tools, and other precision tooling components used across various manufacturing and fabrication processes. We focus on delivering products that ensure accuracy, durability, and efficient performance.
